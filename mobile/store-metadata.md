## Full Description:

Igolo Interior is the all-in-one project management app built exclusively for interior design professionals. Whether you are a business owner, sales executive, site supervisor, or client, Igolo Interior gives you the tools you need to manage every stage of a project, from first contact to final handover.

**For Sales & Business Development**
Capture and qualify leads on the go. Log calls, meetings, and site visits directly from your phone. Create professional, itemised quotations with room-by-room breakdowns and share polished PDF proposals with clients in seconds. Track your entire sales pipeline with status filters so no opportunity slips through the cracks.

**For Project Managers**
Convert accepted quotations into fully structured projects with a single tap. Each project is automatically populated with six standardised execution sprints covering design, civil work, MEP, carpentry, finishing, and handover. Monitor timelines at a glance, manage variation orders, and keep every stakeholder aligned on progress and deadlines.

**For Site Supervisors**
Log daily progress with photos, notes, and blocker reports. Mark attendance for labour teams and submit material indent requests directly from the site. Everything syncs in real time so the back office stays informed without a single phone call.

**For Clients**
Stay connected to your project through a dedicated client portal. View real-time sprint progress, browse daily site photos, and make milestone payments securely from within the app.

**Financial Control Built In**
Every project operates as its own financial wallet. Track money in from client payments and money out to vendors, labour, and petty cash. Built-in spending locks prevent overspend, and real-time dashboards highlight burn rate, margin health, and profitability at a glance.

**Key Features**
- Lead capture and CRM pipeline management
- Room-by-room quotation builder with markup calculations
- PDF quotation generation and sharing
- Automatic sprint generation with dependency tracking
- Daily site logs with photo uploads
- Labour attendance and payroll tracking
- Material indent and purchase order workflows
- Client payment portal with secure transactions
- Role-based access control for every team member
- Offline support with automatic sync when back online
- Push notifications for approvals, payments, and updates
- Biometric login with Face ID and Touch ID

**Roles Supported**
Super Admin, Manager, Business Development Executive, Sales, Supervisor, Client, and Labour Lead. Each role sees only what they need, keeping data secure and workflows streamlined.

Download Igolo Interior today and bring structure, transparency, and efficiency to every interior design project you deliver.
